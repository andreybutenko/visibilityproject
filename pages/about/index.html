About the Visibility Project

The Visibility Project supports public education efforts which lead to interactions, conversations, and relationships between citygoers and Seattle's homeless population.

The Visibility Project tries to approach the isolation issue from two perspective. Citygoers need additional education and encouragement to be willing to approach a homeless individual and have a conversation. Homeless individuals can display their name and needs to indicate they're willing to talk.

This is a design project created by Andrey Butenko, Ephriam Kassa, YoungJin Kim, Abby Tumbleson, and Jennifer Yan at the University of Washington for Informatics 200.
